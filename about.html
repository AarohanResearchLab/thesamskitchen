Meet The Sam

The 'Sam'eer Karambelkar

Hi Folks! I am Sameer Karambelkar, a regular Joe like you all. I have been in Marketing field for 20+ years. I love wandering and exploring. My lust for delicacies made me a foodie. Thus I started experiments in my kitchen and like a aged wine I learned to improvise.
With time I have developed a sense of smell and taste for food like in movie Ratatouille. I am keen to have perfection in dishes I prepare. My love for food made me a teacher too. So here I am with my personal blog, to share my recipes and my experience, to know about your opinions and suggestions. Please enjoy them and if you like them, don't forget to share it with others!

My kitchen is my experiment lab. I try to provide you with short videos and step by step recipes straight from my kitchen experiments. I try to teach interesting and mouth watering food recipes and share with you for your feedback. .
